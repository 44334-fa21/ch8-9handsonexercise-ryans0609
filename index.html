Director(s): Jim Abrahams, David Zucker, Jerry Zucker Wes Anderson

Rating: PG PG
Description: This parody of a plane crash thriller crosses
the world of comedy and suspence when
the crew of flight 209 are stricken with food
poisoning. Ex-pilot, Ted Striker is forced
to help land the plane with the help of many
comedic characters.
Surely they can land the plane...
but don't call him Shirley.		 Mr. Fox is a smart and cunning fox who gave up
his life of crime for his family. Not
satisfied with his life, he plans one last heist
to set his family up for life. Mr. Fox plans to
outwit the local farmers, but gets more than
he bargains for in the process. His friends,
family, and neighbors nust come together to
make the plan come together.
